2023 One-Of-A-Kind Pagani Huayra Dinamica Evo

Specs: Pagani didn’t confirm the vehicle is based on the Huayra Roadster BC but that seems like the most logical donor car here. If that’s indeed the case, we are looking at an open-top supercar with a 6.0-liter AMG twin-turbo V12 engine, developing 802 horsepower at 5,900 rpm and 774 pound-feet (1,050 Newton-meters) of torque between 2,000 and 5,500 rpm. This track-focused machine tips the scales at 2,756 pounds, making it slightly heavier than its coupe derivative and slightly lighter than the non-BC Huayra Roadster.
